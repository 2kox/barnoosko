About barnoosko

barnoosko is a comic company created for people who don’t follow the traditional 9–5 path. Our characters are freelancers, dreamers, gamers, caregivers, students, hustlers — anyone building life in their own way.

We want you to feel seen, laugh a little, and know that your story is worth telling.
